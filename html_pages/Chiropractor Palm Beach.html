4.Should I sleep in a certain position after a chiropractic adjustment?

Yes, sleeping on your back or side with proper pillow support is best. Avoid sleeping on your stomach, as it strains the neck and spine. Using a supportive pillow and mattress helps maintain alignment after an adjustment. Chiropractors often recommend ergonomic sleep positions to enhance recovery and spinal health.

5. What are the peak appointment times for Palm Beach visitors?

Many Palm Beach residents prefer morning and mid-day appointments to avoid peak traffic. Saturday appointments are also popular for those commuting from Palm Beach.
