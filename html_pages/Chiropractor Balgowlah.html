2. Can chiropractic care help improve sleep?

Yes, chiropractic adjustments can relieve pain, reduce stress, and improve nervous system function, leading to better sleep. Spinal misalignments can cause discomfort and tension that disrupts sleep quality.

3. Can chiropractic care help with migraines?

Yes, chiropractic adjustments can reduce the frequency and intensity of migraines by improving spinal alignment and reducing nerve tension. Poor posture, muscle stiffness, and misaligned vertebrae can trigger migraines.

4. Can a chiropractor help with jaw pain (TMJ disorder)?

Yes, chiropractic care can relieve TMJ (temporomandibular joint) disorder pain by adjusting the jaw, neck, and upper spine. Chiropractors also use soft tissue therapy to reduce muscle tightness around the jaw. Patients with TMJ pain often experience less discomfort, improved jaw movement, and reduced headaches after chiropractic treatment.
