Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How can Balgowlah residents easily reach Complete Chiropractic?

We are just 6 km from Balgowlah, with easy access via Sydney Road and Pittwater Road. A short 10–15 minute drive or a quick bus ride will get you to our clinic hassle-free.

2. Can I go to a chiropractor if I have had spinal surgery?

Yes, but chiropractors will modify techniques to ensure safety. They avoid adjusting surgically altered areas but can work on surrounding joints and muscles to improve mobility and reduce compensatory pain. Always inform your chiropractor about past surgeries before treatment.

3. Can I get adjusted too often?

Not usually, but excessive adjustments without proper assessment can overstimulate joints. Chiropractors recommend an appropriate schedule based on your needs. Maintenance care is often once a month or as needed, while acute conditions may require more frequent sessions.

4. Can a chiropractor adjust the whole body?

Yes, chiropractors can adjust not only the spine but also extremities like shoulders, knees, wrists, and ankles. Full-body adjustments help restore joint mobility, improve posture, and reduce pain. Many patients experience relief from chronic pain, stiffness, and injuries through comprehensive chiropractic care targeting multiple areas of the body.

5. Can chiropractic care help with joint cracking or popping?

Yes, if your joints crack frequently, it could indicate joint hypermobility, misalignment, or muscle imbalances. Chiropractors assess the affected joints, improve alignment, and strengthen surrounding muscles to reduce excessive popping. Unlike self-cracking, a chiropractic adjustment targets specific misalignments safely, preventing instability or long-term joint damage.
