FAQ

1. How can Manly residents easily reach Complete Chiropractic?

Manly locals can take the fast ferry (18 minutes) or the regular ferry (30 minutes) to Circular Quay, followed by a 5-minute walk to our clinic. Alternatively, driving via Military Road takes about 30–40 minutes, and buses to the CBD provide additional transport options.

2. How long does a chiropractic session last?

A typical session lasts 15-30 minutes. The first visit is longer (30-60 minutes) because it includes a consultation, examination, and possibly X-rays. Follow-up treatments focus on adjustments and may include soft tissue therapy or exercises.

3. What are common chiropractic issues faced by Manly residents?

Due to the active lifestyle in Manly, common issues include surfing-related back pain, shoulder stiffness from swimming, running injuries, and posture problems from desk work.

4. How soon will I feel results from chiropractic care?

Many patients feel relief immediately after an adjustment, while others notice gradual improvement over several sessions. The speed of recovery depends on the severity of the condition, how long the issue has existed, and overall health.

5. How often should I visit a chiropractor?

It depends on your condition. Acute injuries may require 2-3 visits per week, while maintenance care may be once a month. Chiropractors create personalised treatment plans based on pain levels, mobility, and long-term health goals.
