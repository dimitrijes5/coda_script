4. Can chiropractic care help with fall injuries?

Yes, falls can cause spinal misalignments, joint sprains, and muscle imbalances. Chiropractors assess the spine for hidden injuries and restore normal function through adjustments and rehabilitation. Early chiropractic care can prevent long-term issues like chronic pain, stiffness, or limited mobility from untreated fall injuries.

5.Can chiropractic help with muscle imbalances?

Yes, muscle imbalances occur when one group of muscles is tighter or stronger than its opposing group. This leads to poor posture, joint strain, and movement issues. Chiropractors correct underlying misalignments and prescribe specific exercises to rebalance muscles, improving strength, flexibility, and overall function.
